title: "Dark terminal color schemes in iTerm2 Color Schemes"
description: "Browse 400+ dark background color schemes for iTerm2, grouped by family: Dracula, Nord, Monokai, Gruvbox, Catppuccin, Tokyo Night, and many more."
---

The majority of schemes in this collection use dark or near-black backgrounds. The themes below are drawn directly from the `schemes/` directory of the repository and grouped by visual family or origin. Each name matches the `.itermcolors` filename exactly (minus the extension).

<Note>
  Every scheme listed here has a screenshot in the [GitHub screenshots directory](https://github.com/mbadolato/iTerm2-Color-Schemes/tree/master/screenshots). Filenames follow the pattern `lowercase-hyphenated-name.png`.
</Note>
