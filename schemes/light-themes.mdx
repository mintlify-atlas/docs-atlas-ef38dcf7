---

Light themes use white or off-white backgrounds and are well-suited to bright environments or accessibility needs. The schemes below are drawn directly from the `schemes/` directory of the repository, covering names that include "Light", "Day", "Dawn", "Bright", "Latte", and other light-indicating terms, as catalogued in the repository's README.

<Note>
  Every scheme listed here has a screenshot in the [GitHub screenshots directory](https://github.com/mbadolato/iTerm2-Color-Schemes/tree/master/screenshots). Filenames follow the pattern `lowercase-hyphenated-name.png`.
</Note>
